Quick Troubleshooting Tips (FAQ)

These simple checks can often resolve minor issues instantly, saving you time and the need for a service call. If these steps do not work, please contact us!

This is the most common fix for temporary glitches across almost all electronic and mechanical products: Perform a power cycle. This clears temporary software errors without affecting your saved data.

  1. **Save Your Work:** Close all applications and save any documents you are working on.
  2. **Shut Down:** Click Start/Apple menu and choose Shut Down.
  3. **Wait:** Once fully off, wait 30 seconds.
  4. **Start Up:** Turn the device back on.

If the issue persists, please contact us.

A slow computer might be caused by too many startup programs, low storage, or an outdated OS. However, if you see *unwanted* pop-ups, your homepage changes without your permission, or you get error messages you don't recognize, it is highly likely you have malware or a virus. **Stop browsing immediately** and contact us for professional deep cleaning.

Quick Troubleshooting Tips (FAQ)

Simple checks that might save you a service call.

Perform a power cycle. This clears temporary software errors. Turn it off, unplug it, wait 30 seconds, and plug it back in.

Check your router modem. Unplug the power cable from the back of the router, wait 60 seconds, and plug it back in. Wait 5 minutes for it to reboot.

Disconnect the computer from the internet immediately (unplug ethernet or turn off Wi-Fi). Do not log into bank accounts. Contact us immediately for a deep clean.
